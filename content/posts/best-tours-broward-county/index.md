From its stunning waterways and picturesque beaches to its lively cultural scene, the county is a destination that attracts visitors year-round. Whether you are looking to relax on the beach, explore the local wildlife, or indulge in exciting adventures, Broward County has something for everyone.

With a range of guided tours available, exploring Broward County becomes an enriching experience. Local guides provide insights into the area’s history, culture, and attractions, enhancing your visit. The tours available cater to various preferences, whether you are interested in leisurely sightseeing, thrilling water sports, or informative city explorations.

Broward County boasts a diverse array of tours that showcase its unique offerings. Here are some of the standout options that you can consider during your visit.

### Water Tours

For those who want to experience the beauty of Broward County from the water, the **[Fort Lauderdale](https://tour.techpawz.com/posts/fort-lauderdale-travel-guide/) Millionaire Homes Sightseeing Cruise + Free Drink** offers a delightful experience at $49.83. This tour allows you to cruise through the scenic waterways while enjoying views of luxurious homes, all complemented by a refreshing drink. 

If you're looking for something a bit more adventurous, the **Fort Lauderdale Celebrity Homes Sunset Cruise with Free Drink** is another fantastic option priced at $53.19. As you glide through the water at sunset, you can take in the stunning views while learning about the celebrity residences that dot the coastline.

### Extreme Sports

For adventure travelers, the **Fort Lauderdale Tour with 30 Minute EZ Raider Express Joyride** is an exciting choice at $35. This tour combines a city exploration with a thrilling ride, ensuring an exhilarating experience as you navigate through the area. Additionally, the **Helicopter Tour Above Fort Lauderdale City and Beaches** priced at $92, offers a bird's-eye view of the stunning landscapes and lively city life, making it a memorable experience.

### Bike Tours and Rentals

Cycling enthusiasts will appreciate the **90 min Electric Bike Tour of Fort Lauderdale**, which is available for $69. This guided tour allows you to explore the city at a leisurely pace while enjoying the benefits of an electric bike. For those who prefer to go at their own pace, the **3 Day Electric Bike Rental Bundle** is a great option priced at $299, allowing you to explore Broward County's scenic routes and attractions on your schedule.

## Prices and Deals

Broward County offers a wide range of tours at varying price points, making it accessible for different budgets. The tours range from budget-friendly options starting at $24.74, such as the **Sea Rocket Speed Boat and Sightseeing Tour in Fort Lauderdale**, to premium experiences like the **Fort Lauderdale Sunset Cruise on a 32 Sea Ray** for $440.

For those looking to save, there are several deals available. The **Private Efoil Lesson in Fort Lauderdale** is currently offered at a discounted price of $99, providing a unique water experience at a lower cost. Additionally, the **4 hr Family Bundle Electric Bike Rentals Fort Lauderdale** costs $116, making it a great option for families looking to explore together.

With discounts on various tours, visitors can enjoy the best of Broward County without overspending. Whether you opt for a water tour, an extreme sports adventure, or a leisurely bike ride, there are plenty of options available at competitive prices.

In summary, Broward County stands out as a remarkable destination with its diverse tour offerings, catering to various interests and budgets. From scenic water tours to thrilling adventures and leisurely bike rides, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. As you plan your visit, consider these tours to make the most of your experience in this beautiful part of Florida.
