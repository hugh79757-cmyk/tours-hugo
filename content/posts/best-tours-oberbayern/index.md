Oberbayern features a diverse selection of tours catering to various interests. Here are some of the standout options across different themes:

### City Tours

For those looking to explore the urban landscape, city tours are an excellent choice. One notable option is the **Private Tour Through Munich's Landmarks Legends and Local Life**, priced at $57.64. This tour allows you to discover the city's iconic sites while learning about its long history and lively culture from a local perspective.

Another engaging option is the **2 hour Sights Guided Scrooser Tour in Munich**, available for $67.87. This guided tour combines a unique mode of transportation with sightseeing, offering a fun way to navigate the city's highlights.

### Walking Tours

Walking tours provide an intimate way to experience the sights and sounds of Oberbayern. The **Munich Walking Tour: Self-Guided City Game**, priced at $5.74, allows you to explore the city at your own pace while engaging in a fun, interactive game format. 

Additionally, the **Private Munich Old Town Walking Tour - with optional Breakfast** is a more personalized experience, available for $249. This tour not only covers the historic heart of Munich but also offers the chance to enjoy a meal while soaking in the ambiance of the area.

### Day Trips

For those interested in exploring beyond the city, day trips are available that showcase the natural beauty and historical significance of the region. The **Dachau Memorial Site and Dachau Town Small Group Half-Day Tour**, priced at $66.07, offers a profound look into Germany's history, providing insights into the memorial site and the town itself.

### Specialty Tours

Oberbayern also offers unique experiences for those looking to explore specific aspects of local culture. The **Ghosts of Munich: The Haunted Apprentice Exploration Game**, available for $5.74, is a fun way to learn about the city’s spooky history while engaging in a self-guided adventure. If you're interested in local beverages, the **Private Munich Beer Hall Tour with 3-Course Meal & Beer Tastings** is a premium option at $539, providing an in-depth look at Munich's famous beer culture.

## Prices and Deals

When planning your visit to Oberbayern, it's helpful to know the price range for tours, which varies from budget-friendly options to premium experiences. The price range for tours in Oberbayern spans from as low as $2.35 to $539, catering to various budgets and preferences.

For those seeking premium experiences, the **Private Munich Beer Hall Tour with 3-Course Meal & Beer Tastings** at $539 is a standout, offering A Practical look at Munich's beer culture paired with delicious food.
