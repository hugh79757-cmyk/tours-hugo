El Nozha offers a variety of tours that cater to different interests, from historical explorations to nature experiences. Here are some notable options:

### Day Trips

One of the most popular day trips from El Nozha is the **Day Tour to Giza Pyramids Memphis City and Saqqara Pyramids**, priced at $12. This tour offers an affordable way to explore some of Egypt's most iconic sites, including the Great Pyramids and the ancient capital of Memphis. 

For those interested in a unique experience, the **Half Day Tour To Cave Church of Saint Simon** is available for $32. This tour provides insight into the rich religious history of the area, featuring a visit to one of Cairo's most significant churches, carved into the rock.

Another exciting option is the **VIP Giza Pyramids Sphinx Camel Ride Lunch on The Nile**, priced at $42. This tour combines a camel ride near the Pyramids with a delightful lunch on the Nile, offering a memorable way to experience the wonders of ancient Egypt.

If you're arriving in Cairo and have limited time, the **Cairo Stopover Tours Visit Giza Pyramids from Cairo airport** for $44 is an excellent choice. This tour allows travelers to see the Pyramids without the hassle of extensive planning.

For a more leisurely exploration of the city, consider the **4-Hour Walking and Horse Carriage City Tour of Cairo**, priced at $48. This tour offers a unique perspective of Cairo's streets, blending history with the charm of horse-drawn carriages.

### Multi-Day Tours

For those seeking a more in-depth experience, El Nozha offers multi-day tours. The **Top Overnight To [Luxor](https://daytrips.techpawz.com/posts/luxor-day-trips/) From Cairo** is available for $312, allowing travelers to explore Luxor's ancient temples and tombs while enjoying the comfort of overnight accommodations.

For a more comprehensive journey, the **4 Days Cairo and Luxor Tour Package with Airfare & Accommodation** is priced at $1036. This package includes flights and lodging, making it a convenient option for travelers looking to explore both cities extensively.

## Prices and Deals

El Nozha provides a range of tours that accommodate various budgets. The price range for tours spans from $12 to $2280, ensuring that travelers can find something that fits their financial plan.

For those willing to invest in a premium experience, the multi-day tours offer extensive exploration of Egypt's historical sites, with packages starting from $312.
