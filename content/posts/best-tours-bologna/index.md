Known for its medieval architecture, lively student life, and renowned food scene, Bologna offers visitors a unique blend of experiences that can be best appreciated with the guidance of a knowledgeable local. Exploring the city's winding streets, historic squares, and busy markets with a guide not only enhances your understanding of its heritage but also allows you to discover the authentic flavors and stories that define this remarkable city.

With a variety of tours available, visitors can delve into Bologna's artistic treasures, local dishes, and local traditions. Whether you're interested in cooking classes, food tours, or cultural excursions, a guided experience can provide insights that enrich your visit and create lasting memories.

When it comes to exploring Bologna, there is a diverse selection of tours that cater to different interests. Here are some of the best options available:

### Culinary Experiences

Bologna is famously known as the food capital of Italy, and what better way to experience this than through its culinary tours? The **Bologna Local Pasta Cooking Class, Ragu, Spritz, Wine & Gelato** invites participants to learn the art of pasta-making while enjoying local beverages and desserts for $70.81. This interactive experience allows you to create authentic dishes and savor the flavors of Bologna.

Another excellent choice is the **Bologna: Pasta and Tiramisu Small Group Cooking Class with Wine**, also priced at $70.81. This class combines the joy of cooking with the pleasure of tasting, as you prepare two iconic Italian dishes alongside a selection of wines.

For those who prefer a guided exploration of the local food scene, the **Bologna Food & Market Tour with Local Dishes, Pasta, Wines & More** offers a delicious journey through the city's markets and eateries for $77.77. This tour showcases a variety of local ingredients and dishes, providing a well-rounded taste of Bologna's culinary landscape.

### Walking Tours

Another way to discover Bologna is through its walking tours. The **Bologna City Walking Tour** is a budget-friendly option at $32.36. This tour takes you through the historic city center, highlighting significant landmarks and sharing the long history of Bologna.

For a more immersive experience, consider the **Bologna Walking Food Tour and Highlights with a Local Guide** for $82.81. This tour combines sightseeing with food sampling, allowing you to enjoy both the sights and tastes of Bologna in one delightful outing.

### Day Trips and Specialty Tours

If you’re looking to explore beyond the city, Bologna offers a range of day trips. The **Day Tour Parmigiano, Ferrari Museum, Balsamic, Lambrusco, Lunch with Transfer** is A Practical experience priced at $204.02. This tour includes visits to local producers and attractions, providing a deeper understanding of the region's culinary heritage.

Another notable option is the **Save! Private Day Tour & Lunch: Parmesan, Wine, and Vinegar Tasting**, available for $213.86. This tour focuses on the production of some of Italy's most famous products, offering tastings and insights from local experts.

## Prices and Deals

Bologna's tour offerings are available at a range of prices, catering to different budgets and preferences. The tours mentioned earlier reflect the diversity of experiences available, with prices starting as low as $32.36 for the **Bologna City Walking Tour** and going up to $971 for more exclusive experiences like the **One Way Transfer from Bologna to [Zermatt](https://transfers.techpawz.com/posts/zermatt-transfers/) (Tasch)**.
